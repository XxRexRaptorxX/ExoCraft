## üìñ Description

This Titanfall / Anthem inspired mod adds exosuits with different cool effects to the game!
You can mix different pieces to get the perfect effects!

-----

### Atlas:

**1 armor piece:** Speed I

**2 armor pieces:** Speed I + Strength I

**3 armor pieces:** Speed I + Strength II

**4 armor pieces:** Speed I + Strength II + Resistance I

 -----

### Legion:

**1 armor piece:** Strength I

**2 armor pieces:** Strength I + Haste I

**3 armor pieces:** Strength II + Haste II

**4 armor pieces:** Strength III +Haste III

----- 

### Ion:

**1 armor piece:**  Haste I

**2 armor pieces:** Haste I + Fire Resistance I

**3 armor pieces:** Haste II + Fire Resistance I

**4 armor pieces:** Haste II + Fire Resistance I + Water Breathing I

 -----

### Stryder:

**1 armor piece:**  Haste I

**2 armor pieces:** Haste I + Speed I

**3 armor pieces:** Haste II + Speed II

**4 armor pieces:** Haste II + Speed II + Jump Boost I

 -----

### Ogre:

**1 armor piece:**  Resistance I

**2 armor pieces:** Resistance I + Strength I

**3 armor pieces:** Resistance II + Strength II

**4 armor pieces:** Resistance III + Strength II

 -----

### Features:

- many different armor sets with their own effects
- wear a complete armor set to enhance the effects or mix different pieces to get the perfect effects!
- upgrade them with modules to get even more features
- wide compatibility with other tech mods
- highly configurable
- emissive textures
- can be used either with or without energy [FE]

## üóÉÔ∏è Dependencies

### Supported:
- **Just Enough Items (JEI)**
  - Provides some additional info pages and shows all recipes
  - [CurseForge][cf_jei] | [Modrinth][mr_jei]
- **MoreMcmeta Emissive Textures**
  - Enables the emissive textures of the mod
  - [CurseForge][cf_mcmeta] | [Modrinth][mr_mcmeta]
- **Continuity**
  - Enables the emissive textures of the mod
  - [CurseForge][cf_continuity] | [Modrinth][mr_continuity]
- **Shimmer**
  - Enables colored lightning
  - [CurseForge][cf_shimmer] | [Modrinth][mr_shimmer]
- **Just Enough Effect Descriptions (JEED)**
  - Provides mob effect descriptions in JEI
  - [CurseForge][cf_jeed] | [Modrinth][mr_jeed]
- **Energized Power**
  - Matrix recipes for the assembling machine
  - [CurseForge][cf_ep] | [Modrinth][mr_ep]
- **Industrial Reborm**
  - Matrix recipes for the scrap box
  - [CurseForge][cf_ir] | [Modrinth][mr_ir]
- **Ad Astra**
  - The exo suits are suitable for use in space
  - [CurseForge][cf_aa] | [Modrinth][mr_aa]
- **Epic Fight**
  - Expands the combat mechanics
  - [CurseForge][cf_epicfight] | [Modrinth][mr_epicfight]
